Cardiology

At HealthPlus Hospital, our cardiology department offers comprehensive,
cutting-edge treatments for heart-related conditions.
We specialize in diagnosing and managing a range of
cardiovascular diseases, including coronary artery
disease, heart failure,arrhythmias, and hypertension.

We provide personalized treatment plans, which may
include medication management, lifestyle changes,
and interventional procedures like angioplasty or
heart surgery, all aimed at improving patient outcomes
and quality of life.

Urology

Our Urology department provides expert care for patients with
conditions affecting the urinary system and male reproductive health. Our specialized team of urologists offers a comprehensive range of treatments, addressing concerns such as Kidney stones, Urinary tract infections, Prostate health, Bladder disorders, Male reproductive health.

Our experienced urologists are dedicated to diagnosing and treating a wide array of conditions, ensuring patient comfort and quick recovery.With cutting-edge technology and personalized care, our goal is to provide the highest quality of urological treatment, ensuring optimal patient outcomes

Orthopaedics

Orthopedics at HealthPlus Hospital focuses on diagnosing, treating,
and managing conditions related to the musculoskeletal system,
including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Our
team of highly skilled orthopedic surgeons and specialists offers
comprehensive care for a wide range of issues, from fractures and
sports injuries to complex joint replacements and spine disorders.

The key services we are included as Fracture management,joint
replacements,spine surgery,arthroscopy, pediatric Orthopedics,
rehabilation of physiotherapy.

Neurology

Our Neurology department is dedicated to diagnosing and treating conditions that affect the nervous system. Our team of experienced neurologists offers advanced treatments for a wide range of neurological disorders, including Like Stroke, Epikepsy, Multiple sclerosis, Headache and migraine disorders, Neuromuscular diseases

Our hospital is equipped with cutting-edge technology such as MRI, CT scans, and neurophysiology labs for accurate diagnosis and monitoring. We focus on providing personalized care, combining medical expertise with compassionate support for our patients and their families
